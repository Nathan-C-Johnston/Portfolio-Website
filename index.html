About Me
Since 2016 Nathan has worked as a Project Accountant for EMG with a focus on preparing financial data and reports. Prior to his seven years in property management with EMG, Nathan's accounting career includes serving as a Financial Management Specialist in the U.S. Army Reserve, and auditing for a local accounting firm. As a member of the accounting team Mr. Johnston also performs internal audits, prepares analytical data for property owners and investors, and assists with detailed spreadsheet creation and automation. Nathan earned his Bachelor of Science degree in accounting from the University of Utah.
